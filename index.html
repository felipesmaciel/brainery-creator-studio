Creator-controlled TikTok workflow

Prepare the video here. Make the final post decision in TikTok.

Brainery Creator Studio is a desktop workspace for original educational videos. It helps a creator select a finished video, check its caption and source record, and send it to the creator's TikTok inbox for final editing and posting.

01

Connect the account

The creator authorizes their own TikTok account and confirms the account shown before any upload begins.

02

Review one video

The creator selects one finished original video and reviews the proposed caption before expressly confirming the transfer.

03

Finish in TikTok

TikTok sends an inbox notification. The creator can adjust the cover, caption, visibility, and publishing choice in TikTok.

Creator control comes first. Brainery Creator Studio does not scrape TikTok, access other accounts, or automatically publish videos.
Independent creator tool. Not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by TikTok.
