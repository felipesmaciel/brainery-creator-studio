Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

What this policy covers

This policy covers the Brainery Creator Studio desktop workflow and its public informational website.

Information used

When you connect TikTok, the desktop workflow may receive the TikTok account information and authorization tokens needed to identify the connected creator account and send a creator-selected video to TikTok. It also uses the local video file, title or caption text, and upload status necessary for the requested transfer.

How information is used

Information is used only to show the connected creator account, prepare and send the video transfer you expressly confirm, and report the resulting upload status. The public website does not provide user accounts, analytics, advertising, or a public database of uploads.

Storage and sharing

Authorization credentials are stored locally on the creator's device, not in this public website or repository. We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising. Information is shared with TikTok only when you explicitly authorize and confirm an upload. TikTok processes that information under its own privacy policy.

Retention and deletion

You can disconnect the app through TikTok and remove local credential data from your device. You may request help with a data question through the contact channel below.

Children

This tool is not directed to children under 13.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy by changing the effective date above. For privacy questions or deletion assistance, use the Brainery Creator Studio GitHub issue tracker.
